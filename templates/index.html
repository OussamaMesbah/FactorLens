How It Works
  1. Upload & Parse — FactorLens reads your fund factsheet PDF to extract the fund's investment style, geography, and asset class.
  2. Factor Decomposition — Daily fund returns are regressed against a curated set of factor ETFs (Value, Momentum, Quality, Low-Risk, Size) using rolling OLS.
  3. Investable Replication — Portfolio weights are solved via constrained optimisation to minimise tracking error while keeping weights long-only and summing to 100%.
  4. Quarterly Rebalancing — Weights are updated every quarter. You can see exactly what was bought, sold, or held.
  5. Performance Dashboard — Compare NAV, drawdown, Sharpe ratio, and correlation between the original fund and the factor replica.

Methodology based on Brooks, Tsuji & Villalon (2019), "Superstar Investors" , Journal of Investing.
